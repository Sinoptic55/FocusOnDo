## Key Concepts

- Pomodoro TMS combines task management with time tracking
- Central element: always-visible Pomodoro timer
- Three task views: Lists, Date board, Status board
- Hierarchical tasks with steps
- Automatic time tracking during work intervals
- Smart features: quick capture, morning ritual, weekly review
